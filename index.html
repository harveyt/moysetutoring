Moyse Tutoring
English and Social Studies

Moyse Tutoring works alongside high school and college students that need assistance with English or Social Studies. It aims to provide a nurturing and caring environment where young adults and teenagers receive custom-fit tuition. It is a place where tutoring is skilled, knowledgeable and professional, and where students have the opportunity to flourish.

Many students require extra help with their courses at one time or another: it may be that they are struggling with a particular aspect of the subject, or have a demanding project to complete or exam to write. They might be preparing for further education, or need help composing an essay or paper. Some need just a couple of sessions, some may require several more. Moyse Tutoring works with each student to find the best resources and the most appropriate teaching and learning approaches to help them achieve their objectives, whilst keeping parents/guardians appraised of progress.

All tuition takes place in a designed-for-purpose tutoring room centrally located on South Granville Street at 11th Avenue. It is a space that that allows students to feel comfortable and at home, but that encourages them to focus on the task in hand, to make the very most of the time given. All materials are of the best quality, and wholesome snacks and hot drinks/water are always available (especially for those students who have just finished their school day). The South Granville Street location is ideal for transportation links, as well as a great place for parents to shop or get a coffee whilst their children have their sessions.

Tuition is from 3.30pm to 8.30pm on Monday, Wednesday and Thursday evenings, and from 11am to 6pm on Saturdays. Sessions can be one-on-one, or for a group of up to four. Tutoring is also available as needed during the school holidays, and at other times by arrangement. Students are initially assessed by a free 20-30 minute consultation. In this way, parents and students can get to know Moyse Tutoring, and further sessions can be prepared ahead of time. There are no formal contracts or obligations: tuition works best when there is a foundation of trust built between student, tutor and parents.

Su Thompson
Tutor

Su is originally from London, United Kingdom. After gaining her Bachelor’s degree at the University of Sheffield, Su worked for several years in sales and marketing. Feeling that she wanted a more fulfilling career, she re-trained as a secondary school teacher at the Institute of Education (University of London). Su then went on to teach at three public schools, before emigrating to Canada with her husband in 2004.

For two years, Su worked at a private secondary school in Vancouver, teaching Geography 12, Planning 10 and Social Studies 11. She then decided to pursue post-graduate studies, and gained her Master’s degree (Curriculum and Pedagogy), from the University of British Columbia in 2013.

Since then, Su has worked as a tutor in English and Social Studies for a small independent academy, where she has gained a reputation as a much sought-after and innovative educator. Su has a very clear perspective on how tutoring should be directed, and firmly believes that there is a need for exceptional, high quality tuition that is designed for the individual student.

Pricing

Tuition is $40 per hour for one-on-one, including taxes, payable by cheque or cash at each session.

Each additional student (up to four students total) is $10; two students = $50, three students = $60, four students = $70 total.

Su also donates a percentage of her time for pro-bono tuition.
