Moyse Tutoring

Private tutoring is a growing endeavour in Vancouver: many high school and college students benefit from extra tuition. It may be that they struggle with a particular subject or have a demanding project to complete; or that they are preparing for further education, or need help composing an essay. Some need a couple of sessions, some require many more. But all need a skilled tutor who will come alongside them, to help them through.

Moyse Tutoring will offer after school, Saturday and school holiday tutoring, for one or two students at a time, at the company’s tutoring room located in the Kitsilano/Granville Island neighbourhood. Both English and Social Studies will be offered. Moyse Tutoring is a sole proprietorship owned and operated by Su Thompson.
