Conversation

I’m tired.
            I’m tired, too.
I’m tired.
            I’m sorry for that.
I’m tired.
            I’m doing my best.

I’m doing my best, too.
            I know you work hard.
I don’t think you do.
            I struggle to understand you.
I know who you are.
            I don’t think you do.

I can’t say what you need.
            I don’t need you to.
I can’t see what you see.
            I don’t see much.
I hoped you’d be brighter.
            I know that you do.

I’m ill and I’m tired.
            I’m sorry for that.
I want one day of wellness
            I wish I could give it
I don’t think it’s fair.
            I don’t, either.

I’m glad that you’re happy.
            I wasn’t for a long time.
I’m glad you persevered, then.
            I learned a lot from you.
I’m glad you take after me.
            I’m tired.
